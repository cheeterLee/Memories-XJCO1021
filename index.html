About this website

I was once a mainland Chinese student who majored in Finance at National Central University(Taoyuan City), and I also annually took a trip to Taiwan between 2018 to 2020. This website displays scenary photos of Taiwan as well as entertainment recommendations according to my previous experience.

It was after Covid-19 where all mainland Chinese can no longer travel to Taiwan, and I'm making this website to reminisce some of the good old days there. If you also want to share some of your memories, feel free to contact me through email or simply leave a message at the contact section in this page :)
